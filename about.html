About Our Project

Project Title

Agritech IoT: Dashboard for IoT in Farms and Agriculture

Objective

Our Goal is to help farmers save electricity and resources in agriculture by connecting sensors and identifying different factors that affect the efficiency of the their equipment. It also helps farmers understand easily as it is a simple design, has different language options, etc.

How It Works

Multiple Sensors measure data and send it to a server from which our website reads the data. In our model, the DHT11 sensor measures temperature and humidity. The Arduino Uno R4 sends this data to ThingSpeak using WiFi. Our website then reads that data and displays it live.
